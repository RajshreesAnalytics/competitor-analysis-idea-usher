# Idea Usher — Competitor Analysis Report

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a detailed competitor analysis of Idea Usher, 
a leading software development and digital marketing agency, 
compared against 4 major competitors in the Indian IT market.

## 🎯 Purpose
- Identify key competitors and their strengths
- Analyze market gaps and opportunities
- Provide strategic recommendations for business growth

## 🏢 Companies Analyzed
| Company | Specialization | Threat Level |
|---|---|---|
| Capital Numbers | Data & Cloud Engineering | 🟡 Medium |
| Trigma | AI & ML Solutions | 🔴 High |
| ValueCoders | IT Outsourcing | 🟡 Medium |
| MobileCoderz | Mobile App Development | 🟢 Low |

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Internet Research
- Business Analysis Framework

## 📊 Key Findings
- Idea Usher's Blockchain expertise is a unique differentiator
- Trigma poses highest threat due to strong AI/ML capabilities
- UAE market is an untapped opportunity
